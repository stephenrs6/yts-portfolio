About

Stephen is a Full-Stack Web Developer who loves learning new langauges. Finding creative solutions for sophisticated problems and writing clean, efficient code are things that inspire him. He utilizes his knowledge of human-computer interactions to design User Interfaces that come intuitively for the end user. While he is fully capable of solo-building complex websites with full database back-end all the way from mock-up to roll-out, he thrives much more working in multi-functional, mutually-empowering, culturally-diverse teams.

After obtaining his Bachelor's in Psychology from UC San Diego in 2016 and becoming a Mental Health Advocate, he realized he had an itch for programming, which drove him to completing a Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at UCSD Extension in 2018. Today, he codes at Traffic Roots, maintaining and updating their website applications using (mostly, but not limited to): AngularJS, PHP, Laravel, Bootstrap, Wordpress, JQuery, and MySQL. Aside from programming languages, Stephen is also fluent in Mandarin Chinese at a native-speaker level.

Train Scheduler

Similar to a jumbotron at the train station, this real-time database application incorporates Moment.js and a Google Firebase to host arrival and departure data. Provides real-time information about various trains, namely their arrival times, and time-to-arrival from current time. Users may enter additional trains to track, with changes being reflected on the page in real time. The application was built using Google Firebase, Bootstrap, jQuery, and MomentJS.

Live Music Finder

A search engine for upcoming music events and concerts in your area. Presents users with an interface that searches for and displays artists performing live in the area, as well as similar artists.
