About

Stephen is a Full-Stack Web Developer who loves learning new languages. Finding creative solutions for sophisticated problems and writing clean, concise, efficient code are things that inspire him. He utilizes his knowledge of human-computer interactions, business acumen, and psychology background to hollistically examine his client's business needs, and custom-design User Experiences that come intuitively for the end user. While he is fully capable of solo-building complex websites with full database back-end all the way from mock-up to roll-out, he thrives much more working in multi-functional, mutually-empowering, culturally-diverse teams.

After obtaining his Bachelor's in Psychology from UC San Diego in 2016 and becoming a Mental Health Advocate, he realized he had an itch for programming, which drove him to completing a Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at UCSD Extension in 2018. An internship at Traffic Roots later, maintaining and updating their website applications using technologies such as AngularJS, Laravel, Bootstrap, Wordpress, and jQuery, he now moonlights as a Freelance Web Developer while taking online courses in Computer Science fundamentals. Aside from programming languages, Stephen is also fluent in Mandarin Chinese at a native level.
